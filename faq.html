QuickLinks

FAQ'S

Find answers to your questions about QuickLinks, including how to use the app, its features, and how it can enhance your bookmarking experience.

QuickLinks is a bookmark management tool designed to help you organize and manage your favorite web links efficiently. The application allows you to create categories, add bookmarks with custom icons, edit existing links, and delete unnecessary ones. All your bookmarks are stored in your browser's local storage, ensuring they persist across sessions.

To add a new bookmark, click the "+" button on the home page. A modal will appear where you can enter the link's name, URL, and select a category. You can also upload an icon to personalize your bookmark. To edit a bookmark, click the edit icon on the bookmark card, make your changes, and save. To organize bookmarks, simply assign them to different categories during the creation or editing process.

Categorizing your bookmarks allows you to easily find and manage links based on their relevance. For instance, you can have separate categories for Work, Personal, Entertainment, etc. This organization helps in keeping your bookmarks clutter-free and makes it easier to locate specific links when needed.

QuickLinks includes a light/dark mode toggle, allowing you to switch between themes based on your preference or environment. You can find this toggle in the app's sidebar. The chosen mode will be applied across all pages of the application, providing a comfortable viewing experience whether you're working during the day or at night.

QuickLinks currently saves bookmarks in the browser's local storage, which means your bookmarks are only available on the device where you created them. To access your bookmarks on another device, you would need to manually transfer the data or use browser synchronization features.

Your bookmarks in QuickLinks are stored locally on your device, which means they are not uploaded to any server, ensuring your data remains private and secure. However, this also means if you clear your browser data, you will lose your bookmarks unless you have backed them up.

If you encounter any issues or bugs while using QuickLinks, please contact our support team via the Support section in the sidebar. Providing detailed information about the issue will help us resolve it quickly. You can also check for updates or new releases that may address the issue.
